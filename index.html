Ai Ping Pong Game

Instructions

  1. Go 3-4 feet away from the laptop, and keep it straight.
  2. To move the paddle up, move your right wrist up.
  3. Similiarly, to move the paddle down, move your right wrist down.
  4. Just try to hit the ball with your red paddle, as the computer will always hit it back.
